# 💼 E1 — Venture / Product

## Focus
Venture design, product development, MVP building, and market validation.

## Active Research Lines
- **Orbit Canvas** — venture model framework for early-stage startups
- **MVP Lab** — minimum viable product sprint methodology
- **Product-market fit** — validation frameworks and metrics
- **Founder personality** — matching founder type to venture strategy

## Tools
- [set-method](https://pypi.org/project/set-method/) — classify projects, score ventures, recommend quests
- [Founder Quiz](https://k-venture-studio.vercel.app/matchmaker.html) — discover your founder personality

## Starter Tasks
1. Take the Founder Quiz to discover your type
2. Use `set_method.classify()` to map your venture idea to SET spheres
3. Score your venture with `set_method.score()` using ORBIT framework
4. Build your Orbit Canvas
