# R1a Translational Audience Segments

## Question

Which audience groups are most distinct and strategically relevant for K R&D Lab outputs across science, tooling, and public-facing research artifacts?

## Scope

This starter study maps audience segments and their needs without claiming validated market demand.

## Method

1. List the main output types produced across `SCIENCE`, `ENTREPRENEURSHIP`, and `TECHNOLOGY`.
2. Group likely audiences by use case, literacy level, and validation expectations.
3. Compare where messaging, interface design, and evidence requirements differ.
4. Identify the segments that deserve deeper positioning work.

## Candidate audience groups

- researchers and wet-lab collaborators
- translational innovation programs and ecosystem actors
- founders or operators looking for structured decision support
- students and self-directed learners using explainable research tools

## Status

Starter scaffold for audience segmentation and messaging strategy work.
