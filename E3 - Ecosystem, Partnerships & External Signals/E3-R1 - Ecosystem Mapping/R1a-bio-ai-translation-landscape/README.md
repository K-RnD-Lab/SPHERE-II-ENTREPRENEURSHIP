# R1a Bio-AI Translation Landscape

## Question

Which ecosystems, conferences, partners, and open communities matter most for translating K R&D Lab work across science, tooling, and applied public cases?

## Scope

This starter study is an ecosystem map, not a partnership commitment list.

## Method

1. Map visible actors across bioinformatics, diagnostics, AI tooling, open science, and translational research.
2. Separate actors by role: conference, lab, platform, vendor, investor, community, or collaborator.
3. Track where the strongest overlap exists with K R&D Lab capabilities.
4. Highlight ecosystems that are worth active monitoring.

## Example signal groups

- nucleic-acid therapeutics and delivery communities
- biomarker and diagnostics ecosystems
- research tooling and open-science infrastructure networks
- founder, accelerator, and translational innovation programs

## Status

Starter scaffold for landscape work and signal monitoring.
