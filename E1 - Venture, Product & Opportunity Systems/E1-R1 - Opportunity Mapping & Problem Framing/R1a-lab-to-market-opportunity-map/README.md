# R1a Lab-to-Market Opportunity Map

## Question

Which translational opportunity spaces are the most plausible first commercial or collaboration paths for outputs generated inside K R&D Lab?

## Scope

This starter study maps possible routes from research output to external use without claiming product-market fit.

## Method

1. Identify reusable assets coming from science and technology lanes.
2. Group them by likely user type, pain point, and validation burden.
3. Compare open-signal demand, operational complexity, and time-to-proof.
4. Produce a shortlist of opportunity spaces worth deeper validation.

## Candidate output areas

- research tooling for biomedical hypothesis generation
- public-facing literature and evidence dashboards
- translational analytics support for biomarkers or RNA workflows
- education-facing or founder-facing research explainers

## Status

Starter scaffold. No market claim is treated as validated until a dedicated report and external evidence are added.
