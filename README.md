# SPHERE-II-ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Applied research lane for decision-making, venture design, market sensemaking, ecosystem mapping, and public-case documentation across K R&D Lab.

## What belongs here

- translating scientific or technical capability into opportunity maps
- market, audience, and positioning research
- ecosystem and partner landscape analysis
- public cases that document operating logic, learning systems, and visible experiments

## Tracks

- `E1` Venture, Product & Opportunity Systems
  Opportunity framing, venture logic, product direction, and decision systems.
- `E2` Market, Audience & Behavioral Intelligence
  Segmentation, messaging, market signals, and behavioral patterns.
- `E3` Ecosystem, Partnerships & External Signals
  Ecosystem mapping, partner landscapes, and open-signal monitoring.
- `E4` Applied Investigations & Public Cases
  Systems-facing public cases, workflow studies, and visible learning artifacts.

## Boundary rule

Use `SPHERE-II` when the core output is a decision model, market-facing interpretation, ecosystem view, or public case. Keep scientific claims in `SPHERE-I` and reusable tooling in `SPHERE-III`.

## Standard study structure

- `README.md` — question, scope, and method
- `report.md` — findings, caveats, and next actions
- `figures/` — visuals or framework diagrams when needed
